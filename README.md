# Netflix-Content-Analysis-Dashboard-Project
This project analyzes Netflix's content library using Power BI to uncover trends in content growth, genre distribution, country-wise content production, ratings, and content types. The dashboard provides actionable insights into Netflix's content strategy and global expansion.

Objectives
Analyze Netflix's content distribution across Movies and TV Shows.
Identify trends in content additions over time.
Explore genre popularity and content ratings.
Understand country-wise content contribution.
Visualize key business metrics through an interactive dashboard.

Key Insights
Movies dominate Netflix's content library.
Significant growth in content additions after 2015.
Drama and International Movies are among the most popular genres.
The United States contributes the highest number of titles.
TV Shows have shown steady growth in recent years.
